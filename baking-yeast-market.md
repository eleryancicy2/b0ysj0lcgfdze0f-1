<p><p>The baking yeast market primarily consists of Regular Active Dry Yeast and Instant Yeast. Regular Active Dry Yeast requires proofing in warm water before use, allowing bakers to measure and control fermentation. Instant Yeast, on the other hand, can be mixed directly into dry ingredients, making it more convenient and faster in baking applications. Both types cater to different baking preferences, with Instant Yeast gaining popularity for its ease of use, while Regular Active Dry Yeast is favored for traditional recipes.</p></p>
