<p><p>The Oil Water Separator market comprises three primary types: mechanical-based, chemical-based, and membrane-based systems. Mechanical separators utilize physical methods, like gravity and coalescence, to separate oil from water. Chemical separators rely on chemical agents to enhance separation efficiency. Membrane-based systems employ selective permeability using membranes to filter and separate oil from water. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on factors like oil concentration, water quality, and specific environmental regulations, catering to diverse industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
