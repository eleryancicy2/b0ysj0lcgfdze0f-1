<p><p>The die bonder equipment market is segmented into three main types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual die bonders. Fully automatic die bonders offer high-speed production and minimal operator intervention, making them ideal for high-volume manufacturing. Semi-automatic die bonders provide a balance between automation and manual control, allowing for flexibility in production. Manual die bonders require significant operator input, offering low-cost solutions for smaller-scale or specialized applications, where precision and customization take precedence over high throughput.</p></p>
