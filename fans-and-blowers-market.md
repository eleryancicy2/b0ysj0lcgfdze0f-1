<p><p>The fans and blowers market is categorized into three main types: axial fans and blowers, centrifugal fans and blowers, and others. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis and are ideal for low-pressure applications, providing high airflow with minimal resistance. Centrifugal fans generate airflow by converting rotational energy into pressure, suitable for high-pressure applications. The "others" category includes specialized fans and blowers like ducted or mixed-flow types, catering to niche requirements across various industries.</p></p>
