<p><p>The barley market encompasses various products, each serving distinct uses. Quick pearl barley is pre-cooked for swift preparation, while pearl barley is polished and often used in soups and salads. Barley flour is ground from barley grains, ideal for baking and gluten-free recipes. Barley flakes are processed for quick cooking, resembling oatmeal. Barley grits are coarsely ground barley, used in side dishes. Hulless barley is a whole grain type without a tough husk, and hulled barley has the outer hull removed, enhancing digestibility.</p></p>
